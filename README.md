# Elite Snipers - Web Edition

A web-based tribute to the legendary Warcraft III custom game "Elite Snipers". Experience intense sniper combat in a fully 3D environment with fog of war, team-based gameplay, and tactical positioning.

## 🎮 Play Now

Just open `index.html` in your web browser - no installation or login required!

## 🎯 Features

- **3D Tactical Gameplay**: Full 3D terrain with hills, valleys, and obstacles
- **Fog of War System**: Classic RTS-style fog revealing explored areas
- **Team-Based Combat**: Red vs Blue team battles with AI teammates
- **Instant Kill Mechanics**: One-shot sniper gameplay like the original
- **Smart AI Bots**: Bots navigate terrain, seek cover, and engage enemies
- **Special Abilities**:
  - **Windwalk (Q)**: Become invisible and move faster temporarily
  - **Far Sight (E)**: Reveal a distant area of the map
- **Auto-Targeting**: Automatically shoots enemies in your field of view
- **Dynamic Effects**: Massive muzzle flashes, smoke trails, and impact effects

## 🕹️ Controls

- **Left Click**: Move to location or attack target
- **Right Click + Drag**: Pan camera around the map
- **WASD / Mouse Edge**: Scroll the camera
- **Space**: Center camera on your character
- **Q**: Activate Windwalk ability
- **E**: Activate Far Sight ability

## 🏗️ Technology

- **Three.js**: WebGL-based 3D rendering
- **Canvas 2D**: Fog of war rendering
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: No frameworks, just pure web technologies
- **CDN-based**: Instant loading, no build process required

## 🎲 Gameplay

1. Enter your username
2. Choose Red or Blue team
3. Navigate the terrain using click-to-move controls
4. Eliminate enemy players while avoiding their line of sight
5. Use abilities strategically to gain tactical advantages
6. Watch the kill feed to track team performance

## 🌳 Map Features

- **200x200 unit battlefield** with dynamic terrain elevation
- **80 trees** providing cover and line-of-sight blocking
- **40 rock formations** for tactical positioning
- **Scattered walls** creating choke points
- **Team spawn zones** at opposite corners

## 🤖 AI Features

The bots in this game:
- Navigate around obstacles and terrain
- Maintain safe distances from map edges
- Auto-engage enemies within their field of view
- Respect fog of war (only shoot visible targets)
- Use abilities tactically
- Respawn after elimination
